C
Inline Icon-Photos
Each info block gets a small square photo (~64px) next to its heading, acting like an icon. Schedule = drill photo, Location = exterior, Contact = group shot.

Feel: intimate, human. Photos serve as visual wayfinding.
Risk: low-medium — tight crops needed. The map stays unchanged.

D
Cinematic Backdrop
One dramatic full-bleed class photo becomes the section background (heavily dimmed, gold-tinted). Info + CTA float above in translucent bordered cards.

Feel: moody, immersive, strong character.
Risk: higher — requires one killer hero-quality photo and careful contrast. Most different from current design.
